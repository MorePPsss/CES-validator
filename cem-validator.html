Hello! Welcome to the CEM Validator.

You can use the "Select File" button to choose the CEM (Compound Eye Model) you want to validate.

After selecting the corresponding file, press the "Upload" button to begin validation.

If the validation is successful, a check will appear on top of the page and the text box below will display "Valid CEM file!".

If validation fails, a cross mark will appear, specific error messages will be displayed to help you identify the issue.
